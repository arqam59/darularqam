Welcome to

DARUL ARQAM COLLEGE

Darul Arqam Sharee'a and Art's College is a premier educational institute providing high-quality Islamic and contemporary education. Affiliated with Jamia Nooriya Arabia, we prepare religious scholars ('Ulama) for the modern age.

Our students graduate with the prestigious "Faizy" title alongside University degrees (BA/B.Com).

MISSION & VISION

To develop an Islamic educational framework to prepare scholars who acquire, practice, and propagate Islam with excellence in a modern society.

PECULIARITIES

Integrated education ensuring religious mastery and "Faizy" title alongside degrees from recognized universities.
